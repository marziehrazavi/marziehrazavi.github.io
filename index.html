About Me

I am currently a PhD student at École polytechnique fédérale de Lausanne (EPFL), and a research assistant at Idiap research institute. My research interests include automatic speech recognition (ASR) in particular for under-resourced languages, acoustic data-driven grapheme-to-phoneme conversion, and automatic subword unit derivation and pronunciation generation.
